The Research Fellows of the Hopkins Center with the History of Arts at Dartmouth project strive to capture a snapshot of the legacy of Arts at Dartmouth College.

Through archival research and digital presentation, the project seeks to unwind the complex of Dartmouth’s Arts history.

The project observes an inclusive approach to presenting sections of Dartmouth’s Arts history by platforming the under-represented and the marginalized in addition to the more mainstream accomplishments of the institution.

The Research Fellows are committed to presenting Dartmouth’s history as a guiding light for its future endeavors.
